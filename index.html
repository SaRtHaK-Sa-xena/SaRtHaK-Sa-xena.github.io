Custom Physics Library - Carrom

Carrom is a game similar to billards in terms of physics based calculations. The game was built from a custom physics engine created by me through the help of GLM library. The engine allows the physics collision calculations between spheres to spheres, spheres to planes and spheres to boxes.
The local multiplayer game is turn based and is ended by one of the players collecting the most coins
